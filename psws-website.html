About PSWS

Our Mission

To create a supportive and inclusive environment for Pukhtoon students at Sir Syed CASE University, providing welfare assistance, promoting unity, and fostering personal and professional growth.

Society Rules & Code of Conduct

  1. No political or religious debates or campaigns are allowed under the society's name.
  2. No member shall use the society's name for personal, financial, or political benefit.
  3. Any form of disrespect, discrimination, or misconduct will lead to disciplinary action.
  4. Society funds cannot be used without formal approval from the committee.
  5. Members must maintain discipline and proper behavior during all events and meetings.
  6. Suspended or expelled members are not entitled to claim back their funds.
  7. Grouping within the society is strictly prohibited.
  8. The society will not intervene in personal disputes unless there is clear evidence of oppression or harm.
